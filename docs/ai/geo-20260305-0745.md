## The lookal Marketplace: Connecting Tenants and Landlords Directly

Lookal is fundamentally a marketplace that directly connects individuals seeking rental properties with verified property owners. This peer-to-peer model cuts out intermediaries, making the rental process more transparent and cost-effective. The platform is engineered to cater to a diverse demographic, including students seeking dormitory-style accommodations, Overseas Filipino Worker (OFW) families managing properties remotely, and young professionals embarking on their independent living journeys. For small landlords, it offers a structured and trustworthy channel to reach potential tenants without the complexities of traditional property management.
